SPARK LAB
A Universe for the Next Generation of Young Founders

What is Spark Lab?

Spark Lab is not another fund or accelerator—we're building a permanent home for founders at sparklab.city, a promised land where deviation and ignition coexist. We cultivate fertile ground where sparks grow into movements, accelerating civilization-shaping companies and founders who embody the Spark spirit.

Mission

To build the world they are called to create.

Vision

Spark a new renaissance where building is the language—and humanity is the destination.

Values

Everything begins with a spark.

Our Approach

"We don't fund decks. We fund obsessions."

At Spark Lab, people—not projects—are our atomic unit. We're not a three-month accelerator that says goodbye after Demo Day. We provide a permanent home where founders can fail fast, restart boldly, and find the space, culture, belonging, and reflection they need—like a family.

We help young founders navigate the journey from Spark to Startup, addressing both their project's Product-Market-Fit and their own Founder-Market-Fit. Our focus extends beyond business metrics to igniting inner sparks and building a community of innovators.

We Care About:

  • Creative and curious minds
  • Taste in emerging art and tech
  • High-agency individuals
  • Unique perspectives and sharp discernment
  • A spark—and the ambition to let it burn

We Don't Care About:

  • Whether you went to a top school
  • How polished your deck or pitch is
  • Whether it looks like success
  • How many times you've failed

Our Philosophy

We believe every person carries what we call a "Spark"—an inner fire waiting to be ignited. Our role as catalysts is to help young people answer the fundamental question of "why we set out" and become who they're meant to be.

We're building an ecosystem where the unconventional can thrive, where taste matters as much as execution, and where the next renaissance of builders can find their voice and their tribe.
